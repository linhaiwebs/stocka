# AI Stock Technical Analysis Platform

Professional technical analysis information platform providing computational indicator data and chart pattern information for research purposes.

## Features

- Technical indicator calculations (RSI, MACD, Moving Averages, etc.)
- Chart pattern information display
- Multi-timeframe analysis views
- Historical data processing
- Comprehensive risk disclaimers

## Google Ads Compliance

This template is specifically designed to comply with Google Ads policies for financial information services:

### Compliance Features

1. **Informational Purpose**: Clearly states all data is for informational purposes only
2. **No Trading Signals**: Explicitly disclaims providing trading recommendations or signals
3. **Risk Warnings**: Prominent warnings about market risks throughout
4. **Historical Data Disclaimer**: Makes clear that past patterns don't predict future results
5. **No Guarantees**: States no accuracy guarantees for technical calculations
6. **Professional Consultation**: Strongly encourages consulting licensed professionals
7. **User Responsibility**: Makes clear users are responsible for their own decisions

### Key Compliance Disclaimers

- "Informational Only • Not Trading Advice • Past Performance Does Not Predict Future Results"
- "Technical analysis does not eliminate or reduce market risk"
- "Technical indicators shown are computational outputs, not trading recommendations"
- "Always consult licensed financial professionals before any market decisions"

## Target Audience

This template is suitable for:
- Market researchers requiring technical data
- Analysts studying technical indicators
- Information platforms providing market data
- Research tools for financial professionals
- Academic and educational research

## Design Philosophy

The purple/indigo color scheme creates a technical, data-focused aesthetic appropriate for analytical tools. All language emphasizes information provision and data calculation rather than trading success or predictive capabilities.

## Technical Indicators Included

Sample indicators displayed:
- Relative Strength Index (RSI)
- Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
- Moving Averages (various periods)
- Bollinger Bands
- Volume Analysis
- Momentum Indicators

## Important Notes

- All testimonials focus on informational value
- No claims of prediction or guaranteed outcomes
- Clear separation between data provision and trading advice
- Emphasis on computational nature of technical indicators
